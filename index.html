About Me

I am Elsa Kabore, the analytical mind dedicated to transforming raw data into clear, actionable business strategies. As a versatile and detail-driven Business Intelligence & Data Analyst, I focus on crafting visual experiences and clean data models that drive impact.

I specialize in relational database management systems like PostgreSQL, advanced SQL querying, and designing interactive dashboards. I work with organizations to help them shape strong analytical identities, optimize their collection workflows, and improve how they connect insights with decision-making.

My goal is to turn complex datasets into clean, meaningful, and engaging data stories that not only look good but serve a defined strategic purpose.
